Information

Appointments

These are available during surgery hours on weekdays. Appointments are booked through the reception or can be made online at ManageMyHealth. A standard appointment is 15 minutes. If you have multiple problems to manage or think you will need longer please advise our receptionist.

New Patient Enrolment

We welcome new enrolments. Please call into reception and ask about enrolling.

Repeat Prescriptions

Requests for repeat prescriptions are done via the ManageMyHealth patient portal or through reception. You will need to be specific about which medications you are requesting and allow 24 hours for this request to be followed up. There is a charge for this service. If you have not been seen in the preceding 6 months you may be asked to make an appointment with your doctor for a clinical review.

Fees

Payment is required on the day of consultation unless prior arrangements have been made. We accept cash, cheque, internet bill payment, EFTPOS and credit card. There is a merchant fee added if you choose to pay with credit card. Our fee schedule is displayed in the waiting room and at reception. This is also available on the Southern PHO website. Doctors are happy to discuss fees on an individual basis.

Results

Results can be viewed on ManageMyHealth once the doctor has seen and filed them. Results can also be notified by text message. Your doctor or nurse will notify you of any abnormal results that need follow up or further investigation.

Home Visits

When necessary these are usually scheduled during office hours. Please contact the surgery and speak to our nurse and she will discuss your needs with your doctor. Please note home visits will incur an extra fee.
